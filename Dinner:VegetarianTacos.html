Vegetable Tacos

These Vegetarian Tacos are loaded with black beans, squash, corn, and even more roasted veggies! These can easily be made vegan or gluten-free, and the whole family will love this recipe.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Corn
  • Zucchini
  • Yellow squash
  • Red bell pepper
  • Olive and canola oil
  • Spices (cumin, chili powder, salt and pepper)
  • Black Beans
  • Cilantro
  • Lime Juice
  • Tortillas, for serving
  • Taco toppings of choice

Instuctions

  1. Prep the fresh veggies and place on roasting sheet.
  2. Drizzle with oil and sprinkle with spices. Toss to combine.
  3. Roast for 20 minutes, stirring the veggies halfway through.
  4. Once the vegetables have finished roasting, add the black beans, lime juice and cilantro to the pan and stir.
  5. Serve veggie taco filling with warm tortillas and your favorite toppings.

Recipe taken from Cooking Classy .
