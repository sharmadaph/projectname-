Pesto Chicken Bake

four ingredient pesto chicken bake is healthy, simple, delicious, and requires five minutes of prep

Ingredients

  • 3 chicken breats
  • 16 ounce jar basil pesto
  • 2 medium tomatoes, sliced
  • 3 slices mozzarella cheese

Instuctions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Place chicken breats in baking dish
  3. Top each chicken breast with 1 slice of mozzarella cheese and 2 sliced tomatoes
  4. Bake for 30 minutes, or until chicken is no longer pink in the middle. Broil the chicken the final 2 minutes so that the cheese is bubbly and golden.

Nutrition Information

462 Calories 37.7g Fat 3.8g Carbs 27.5g Protein
