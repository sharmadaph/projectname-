Peanut Butter Cinnamon Smoothie

The banana adds a spice taste to the sweet smoothie. This healthy smoothie can be drank in the morning or as a snack. Adding the peanut butter and banana to the milk will add a creamy and enhance the taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup SILK light vanilla soy milk 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
    • 1 each SILK dairy-free vanilla yogurt
  • 3/4 cup raw cocoa nibs
  • 1/2 cup smooth peanut butter
  • 1 medium banana, frozen
  • 4 pitted medjool dates
  • 4 pitted medjool dates
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon

Instuctions

  1. Pour all ingredients into a blender, in the order of the ingredients list.
  2. Puree until smooth or desired consistency is achieved. 1 to 2 minutes.
  3. Pour into serving glasses and top with a sprinkled of cocoa nibs and cinnamon. Serve.
