Citrus Fruit Salad

Citrus fruit salad, perfect for winter citrus, pink grapefruit and navel oranges, sweetened with honey, seasoned with lime juice and cardamom.The cardamom provides a lovely accent to the honey-sweetened citrus segments.

Ingredients

  • 2 large navel oranges
  • 1 large grapefruit
  • 2 cups pineapple (fresh, chopped)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • Garnish: zest of 1 lime

Instuctions

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Cut the oranges and grapefruit into small pieces. Make sure to remove as much of the pith and outer membranes. It will look prettier and taste better this way.
  3. Whisk together the freshly squeezed lime juice, vanilla extract, and powdered sugar. If you have vanilla sugar that can be substituted for the powdered sugar and vanilla extract. Just use 1/4 cup of vanilla sugar.
  4. refrigerator, covered for about twenty minutes before serving.
  5. Serve the fruit salad with vanilla yogurt. You can use greek or regular. Zest a lime and garnish individual bowls with the lime zest.
