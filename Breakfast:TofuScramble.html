Tofu Scamble

Tofu scramble is a delicious vegan breakfast that's loaded with plant-based protein and vegetables. Choosing the right type of tofu is key to achieving a tender scrambled eggs consistency.To make the tofu visually appear similar to eggs, a small amount of turmeric coats the pieces for a sunshine yellow color.

Ingredients

  • 8oz (~220g) Extra Firm Tofk
    • 1 Tbsp Vegan Butter
  • 2 Tbsp Nutritional Yeast
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp Paprika
  • 1 tsp Dijon Mustardr
  • 1/2 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1/4 tsp Black Salt (Kala Namak)
  • 1/4 tsp Onion Powder

Instuctions

  1. Mash the tofu with a fork but leave some nice big chunks.
